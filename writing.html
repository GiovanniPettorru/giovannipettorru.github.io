Why We Must Stop Believing the "False Myth" of Digital Resistance
As a researcher and head of the AGROS project, I spent the last year doing something engineers often forget: listening to the real needs of those who will actually use our solutions. Field validation across Sardinian farms revealed that 87% of farmers are eager to invest in new technology — and zero said they were "not interested at all." The real barriers are cost and complexity, not culture.
