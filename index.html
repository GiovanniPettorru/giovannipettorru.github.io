Biography

Giovanni Pettorru is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Cagliari (DIEE), working on the Horizon Europe project HEAT – Hybrid Extended reAliTy. He holds a Ph.D. in Electronic and Computer Engineering (2026), with research focusing on GPS-free localization, network security, and trustworthy Location-Based Services for next-generation networks.

He has authored over 20 peer-reviewed publications in IEEE journals and conferences. Beyond academia, Giovanni translates technology into impact: his projects AGROS, LENS, and GreenShield have earned national recognition and a Silicon Valley acceleration through the SERICS Lab2Bay program at INNOVIT.

Autore di oltre 20 pubblicazioni peer-reviewed su riviste e conferenze IEEE, Giovanni trasforma la ricerca in impatto concreto: i suoi progetti AGROS, LENS e GreenShield hanno ottenuto riconoscimenti nazionali e internazionali, incluso un percorso in Silicon Valley con il programma SERICS Lab2Bay presso l'hub INNOVIT.

Giovanni Pettorru is currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (DIEE) of the University of Cagliari, where he contributes to the Horizon Europe project “HEAT – Hybrid Extended reAliTy”. His work focuses on high-precision positioning infrastructures and GPS-free localization for immersive XR environments.

He earned his Ph.D. in Electronic and Computer Engineering in February 2026. His doctoral thesis, Energy-Efficient and Trustworthy Location-Based Services for Next-Generation IoT, established his expertise in network security and green IoT strategies. Previously, he received his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (2020) and his M.Sc. in Internet Engineering (2022) from the same university.

Giovanni is an active member of IEEE, recognized for his communication skills as a finalist in the 3MT and 4MT competitions at ICC and Globecom (2023–2025). He serves the scientific community as Web and Social Media Chair for the IEEE ComSoc Italy Chapter and as part of the Organizing Committee for IEEE MeditCom 2026.

His entrepreneurial vision has led to multiple awards: AGROS (Start Cup 2024 top 10), LENS (Start Cup 2025 top 10 & Climate Change mention), and GreenShield, which was recently selected for the exclusive SERICS Lab2Bay acceleration program in Silicon Valley at the INNOVIT hub.
